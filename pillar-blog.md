# Complete Travel Guide to Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is one of India's most diverse tourism destinations. From the sacred hills of Tirupati to the scenic beauty of Araku Valley and the beaches of Visakhapatnam, the state offers unforgettable experiences.

## Why Visit Andhra Pradesh?
- Spiritual tourism
- Hill stations and valleys
- Beaches and coastal attractions
- Rich Telugu culture
- Traditional cuisine

## Top Attractions
### Tirupati
Home to the famous Sri Venkateswara Temple.

### Araku Valley
Known for coffee plantations and breathtaking landscapes.

### Visakhapatnam
Popular for beaches, museums, and family tourism.

### Waterfalls
Talakona and Katiki waterfalls attract nature lovers.

## Travel Tips
Plan trips between October and February for pleasant weather. Use local guides and pre-book accommodations during peak seasons.
